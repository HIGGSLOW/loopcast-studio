Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-01

Loopcast Studio ("the App") is a content-posting tool that lets an authorized user connect their TikTok account and publish video content to it.

Information We Collect

When you authorize the App via TikTok Login Kit, we receive the profile information TikTok provides under the granted scopes (e.g. open id, display name, avatar). We do not collect passwords - authentication is handled entirely by TikTok's OAuth flow.

How We Use Information

We use the authorized access solely to publish video content to the connected TikTok account on the user's behalf, at the user's direction. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored only as needed to perform posting operations and can be revoked at any time by the account holder from their TikTok settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the contact address listed in the App's TikTok Developer listing.
